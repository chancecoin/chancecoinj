Made for dice betting

Chancecoin is specifically designed for betting on dice rolls. Pick your odds and make your bet. Or invest in the house bankroll.

Decentralized

Built on top of the Bitcoin network, Chancecoin is fully decentralized. There is no need for a central casino as a point of failure.

Fair and open

Chancecoins are created during a proof-of-burn time period that is open to everyone. The code itself is open source and published on GitHub.
